## What are the key benefits of using Blue?

Blue offers robust features to help teams organize work and collaborate effectively. Key benefits include customizable workflows, intelligent automation, real-time communication, and powerful analytics.

## How much does Blue cost?

Blue has one plan with all the features.
- Monthly plan: **$7/month/user**.
- Yearly plan: **$70/year/user (so you get two months free)**

These users roles are paid:
- [Project Administrator](/docs/user-management/roles/project-administrator)
- [Team Member](/docs/user-management/roles/team-member)
- [Custom User Roles](/docs/user-management/roles/custom-user-roles)

And these user roles are free:
- [Clients](/docs/user-management/roles/client)
- [Comment Only](/docs/user-management/roles/comment-only)
- [View Only](/docs/user-management/roles/view-only)

## How does the Blue free trial work?

The free trial provides full access to all Blue features for 7 days. No credit card is required to start. If you wish to continue, you can upgrade to our paid plan at the end of the free trial and you will also have an option to extend your trial for another 7 days. 

You can always email us at help@blue.cc if you need a longer trial or you want personalized onboarding.

## What integrations does Blue support?

Blue has 5,000+ integrations via [Pabbly Connect](/docs/integrations/pabbly-connect) and [Zapier](/docs/integrations/zapier). You can find a [list of our integrations here](https://www.blue.cc/platform/integrations).

We also have a [full API for custom integrations](/docs/integrations/api) and granular [Webhooks](/docs/integrations/webhooks).

## Can I import my existing data into Blue?

Yes, Blue supports [bulk data import from CSV files](/docs/integrations/csv-import). This allows you to migrate up to 200,000 records simultaneously and is perfect for migration from manual Excel workflows or other work management systems.  You can reach out to help@blue.cc if you have questions about importing your data or to enquire about paid support packages.

## Does Blue have Mobile Apps?

Yes, [native iOS and Android apps](/docs/start-guide/download-apps) are available to access Blue on the go. Core functionality like tasks, comments, and calendars sync across devices.

## How do permissions work in Blue?

Permissions are set at the project level, so users can have different access levels across projects based on their roles. [Blue has a powerful custom user roles system](/docs/user-management/roles/custom-user-roles) that allows you to define the precise access for each user group in each project.

## Can I customize the interface and workflows in Blue?

Yes, you can completely customize fields, views, automation rules, forms, and more to match your work requirements. You can choose the colour of the interface and upload your company logo. We also allow you to have a custom domain.

Yes, you can export all your Blue data anytime via the [API](/docs/integrations/api) or [bulk CSV Export](/docs/integrations/csv-export). You own your data, and it is always accessible.
