---
title: Copying Projects
description: You can make perfect copies of projects in Blue.
---

You can copy projects within your organisation or to other organisations by clicking on the project settings icon. Only [Project Administrators](/public/docs/user-management/roles/project-administrator) can copy projects.

![Copying Project 1](/public/docs/CopyingProject_1.png "Copying Project 1")

Note that only Project Administrators have access to project settings

When copying projects, you can choose what you want to copy across.

![Copying Project 1](/public/docs/CopyingProject_2.png "Copying Project 2")

Choose which components of a project you want to copy

::callout
---

icon: i-heroicons-light-bulb
---

**Time required to copy projects** <br/>
Depending on the size of your project, copying can take anywhere from a few seconds to a few minutes.
::
